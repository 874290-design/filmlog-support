❓ Frequently Asked Questions

Does NegLog require an internet connection?

No. NegLog works entirely offline. No account, no cloud, no network requests.

Where is my data stored?

All your data is stored locally on your device using Apple's Core Data. Nothing leaves your phone.

What happens if I delete the app?

Your data is deleted along with the app. Please back up your device regularly to preserve your data.

Is there an Android version?

Not at this time. NegLog is currently iOS only.
